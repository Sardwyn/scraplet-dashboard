## Pragmatic Play

The most prolific provider on Stake. Known for high-volatility slots with strong bonus features.

- **RTP**: 96.5% | **Volatility**: Very High | **Max Win**: 5,000x
- **Mechanic**: Tumbling reels, multiplier orbs, Zeus scatter
- **Bonus**: Free spins with persistent multipliers that accumulate
- **Why it's popular**: Consistent big win potential, satisfying bonus mechanic, Zeus theme resonates
- **Gates of Olympus 1000**: Enhanced version with 1000x max multiplier potential in bonus

### Sweet Bonanza
- **RTP**: 96.5% | **Volatility**: Very High | **Max Win**: 21,100x

### Starlight Princess
- **RTP**: 96.5% | **Volatility**: Very High | **Max Win**: 5,000x

- **RTP**: 96.5% | **Volatility**: Very High | **Max Win**: 6,750x

### Wild West Gold
- **RTP**: 96.5% | **Volatility**: Very High | **Max Win**: 5,000x

## Slot Selection Guide

Best slots for entertaining content:
1. High volatility — dramatic swings make better content
2. Clear bonus features — viewers can follow what's happening
3. Big win potential — moments worth clipping
4. **Top picks**: Money Train 3, Gates of Olympus, Wanted Dead or a Wild, Chaos Crew

### For Longer Sessions
Best slots for extended play:
1. Medium volatility — balance lasts longer
2. Frequent small wins — keeps engagement up
3. **Top picks**: Big Bass Bonanza, Book of Dead, Gonzo's Quest

### For Bonus Buys
Best slots when buying directly into bonus:
1. High max win potential — justifies the premium cost
2. Exciting bonus mechanics — worth watching
3. **Top picks**: Money Train 3, Gates of Olympus 1000, Wanted Dead or a Wild
